🎯 About the Program

The ASG Platform 2-Year Residency Program for Software Developers is a prestigious, scholarship-based coding bootcamp designed to transform individuals with solid programming foundations into world-class developers.

⚠️ This is not a beginner program — it's for those who already have hands-on coding background, whether self-taught or from non-traditional paths, and are ready to tackle a rigorous, fast-tracked journey toward elite developer status.

🚀 Path to Career Success

This program is more than just an educational experience — it's a stepping stone to potentially joining the ASG Platform's Developer Team.

🏆 Top performers who demonstrate exceptional skill, dedication, and alignment with our values may be invited to join the team, earning ₱100,000+ per month.
🎁 After the first year, participants gain access to company benefits such as HMO coverage, stock options, paid time off, and entertainment and meal vouchers.
🎯 Joining full-time also brings even more enhanced versions of these perks.

📜 Key Details

💸 Monthly Allowance: ₱32,000–₱35,000 to start, with increases as you progress.
📈 Accelerated Learning: Condenses 5 years of experience into 2 years, with personalized guidance by senior developers.
🌍 Fully Remote: Open to Filipino citizens residing in the Philippines.

  • ✅ Full-Time Commitment: No other jobs, gigs, or major obligations allowed during the program.

  • ✅ Learn-It-All Mindset: Participants must embrace continuous learning and thrive on challenges.
